VOV STANDARD

THE RULES BEHIND THE RESULTS.

VOV Standard defines the methodology, evidence requirements, and governance framework behind every VOV Verified investigation.

Why This Exists

People should not need to cross-check dozens of sources to decide what is trustworthy.

VOV Verified publishes outcomes. VOV Standard publishes the process and guardrails used to produce those outcomes.

Core Pillars

Methodology

How investigations are scoped, researched, documented, and published.

Evidence Standards

Source quality tiers, corroboration requirements, and conflict-resolution rules.

Governance

Independence, accountability, correction policy, and revision controls.

Constitution

Foundational operating principles and non-negotiable commitments.

Trust Mechanics

  • Public evidence logs
  • Transparent decision criteria
  • Correction protocol with timestamps
  • Versioned standards with visible updates

How It Connects to VOV Verified

VOV Verified is the outcome layer. VOV Standard is the rules layer that governs every outcome.

Question -> Investigation -> Evidence Review -> Decision -> Public Record
